Built for African Conditions, Designed for Impact
AQUAtap is more than a water treatment system. It is engineered as a resilient, scalable solution that reduces costs, ensures consistent quality, and empowers communities with sustainable water security.

Smart Prepaid Taps

AQUAtap integrates a cashless prepaid card system, ensuring transparent water distribution. Households, schools, and businesses pay only for what they use — reducing waste, promoting fairness, and making every drop accountable.

Solar-Powered System

With built-in solar panels, AQUAtap runs independently of unstable municipal grids. Even during load-shedding or in off-grid regions, the system guarantees continuous, reliable access to clean water.

Low Operating Costs

Energy recovery technology and solar efficiency lower the lifetime cost per litre, making AQUAtap significantly cheaper than trucking or bottled water. Communities save money while gaining safe, sustainable supply.
